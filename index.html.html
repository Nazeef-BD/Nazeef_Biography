The Biography of

Md. Nazeef Farhan

Birth and Early Years

Md. Nazeef Farhan, a Bangladeshi Muslim boy, was born on November 2nd, 2012 in his hometown Bogura as the first child to his parents. His short name is Nazeef. As a child, he learnt both English and his mother tongue, Bangla simultaneously. He was calm child but needed much attention.

Shifting at the Start

Due to his father's professional commitments, Nazeef's childhood was characterized by frequent relocations across different districts. After his birth his stay was at his maternal grandmother's in Bogura for a few months. Then his family went to Joldhaka in Nilfarmari district to stay for 1 year. After that, they shifted to Sayedpur also in Nilfarmari and stayed almost another year there. Then, the family moved to Dinajpur to stay there for another 1 year. At 3 years old he finally returned to Bogura to stay there with his mother again at his maternal grandmother's house. His mother, a former teacher, again joined her job at the local high-school. Then they shifted again a few times in their locality until they got settled in 2018.

Starting School

A year before getting settled, in 2017, he got admitted into Netpro Model School & College, Bogura in Play level. In a school vacation of 2018, his family went to travel in Kolkata for 5 days. He studied at Netpro Model School & College upto kindergarten level in 2019, the same year his father came back home as he had left his job and had set up a business. This time, Nazeef spent most of his time playing with friends, playing games, drawing and studying . In 2020, he started going to BIAM Model School & College Bogura from class 1 . Due to the worldwide pandemic of Covid-19, his classes were closed from the month of May when in class 1. He had to take online classes on zoom. He even had to give his annual exams of class 1 at home. His online classes also took place in class 2, but his exams were held at school that time. He spent much time watching TV in quarantine. From class 3, his school life was starting to return to being normal and classes were being held in the classroom again. Nazeef started to improve his studies. Class 4 was also normal. But when he was studying in class 5, the July revolution happened and his studies were a bit disturbed, but no big loss happened nonetheless. Then his primary education had been finished. He had started class 6 - the beggining of high-school.

Starting High-School and Cadet-preparation

Nazeef was excited to start the 'new-curriculum' he had heard about, but it was cancelled. Anyways, he started getting normalized to high-school, got into a cadet coaching and started to learn programming at Codingal. Cadet coaching became a big part of his academic life and he started to want to be a cadet . He also became friends with his now-best-friend at coaching. He started to excel in his studies. Around the end of the year 2025(when Nazeef was in class 6), a big exam was held all around Bangladesh by a big coaching center. Most students who wanted to get into Cadet colleges took part in this exam. Nazeef also took part. He became first in the whole of Bogura in this exam. he was very happy because this was the first time he became first in any exam. He also became 18th in the whole of Bangladesh in this exam. Then finally the time for the exam came. When the results came, Nazeef got a chance in the written exam. He was also very excited. He began taking preparation for the viva. Then he took the viva and he went to Cox's Bazar on a family trip right after the viva.

Getting used to High-school

After the final results, neither he nor his best friend got any chance to a Cadet College. Both the boys became sad, but they overcame the sadness. After some self-researching and thinking, Nazeef soon realized that Cadet Colleges might not have been the best decision for him. After some weeks, Nazeef's bestfriend called him and told Nazeef that he(the bestfriend) has gotten a chance to study at Pabna Cadet College as his name was in the candidate waiting list. Nazeef became happy for his bestfriend and also didn't get jealous as he had understood that Cadet Colleges aren't for him. Now, Nazeef is still studying at BMSC and he is focusing to be the best student. He has created goals to study at IIUM and become a software engineer in the future. He is determined to fulfill his plans and is working accordingly.

Current Personality and Interests

Nazeef is a very ambitious boy. He wants to go to Malaysia to study in the International Islamic University of Malaysia (IIUM). He wants to study Software Engineering and Psychology there . Then he will come back to Bangladesh and pursue his career. Nazeef has many interests and passions. Such as - Math, Painting, Science, Geography, Arabic etc. He learns many things about the things he is passionate about. He believes that with determination and curiosity, there is no limit to what a student can achieve
